# Code of conduct

QuotaHalo is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community.

Participants must communicate respectfully, accept constructive feedback, avoid personal attacks or discriminatory language, and keep technical discussions focused on the project. Harassment, threats, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and publishing another person's private information are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is inappropriate, threatening, or harmful to the community.

For sensitive conduct reports, contact the maintainer privately through the repository's GitHub security-reporting flow. Reports will be reviewed fairly and kept as confidential as reasonably possible.
